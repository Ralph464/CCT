"Everything is connected, and it drove me insane."

Chase Krason June 9th, 2022

'"I love Science. Science rules the world. You and I are science. Science is amazing. There are so many concepts in science because everything is science. I wish science was everywhere; oh wait, it is!" Josh Tyson. I really like this quote because it changed how I viewed the world forever. Everything is a ratio, everything is linked, and there is math. This lead me down a rabbit hole in which I looked into the human anatomy (generally how hormones and the human psyche). It completely changed the way I look at people. As a result, it turned me cynical and mad. Nothing truly matters, except for human benefit. Everything is calculated. Everything is a ratio. There is no escape. I am an empty vessel.'

This is the manifesto of Matthew Cramer, "ring leader" of the notorius underground organization "The Cult". The Cult has wreaked havoc in our community due to their activities in the drug trade. Their contributions has caused increasing drug overdose and murder rates. As "The Cult" faces their RICO charge, it seems like the terror and torment that the group inflicted onto the civilians and the police force is coming to a certain end.
